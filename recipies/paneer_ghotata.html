PANEER GHOTALA

DESCRIPTION

an interestingly unique and tasty street style paneer curry recipe inspired by its egg variant. it is basically a vegetarian or no egg alternative recipe prepared with paneer crumbles to have the same taste and texture as anda ghotala recipe. it is generally prepared as street food and served with masala coated pav, but it can also taste great when served with a choice of roti, naan or even rice.

INGREDIENTS

  • 200 grams paneer (grated)
  • 2 tsp oil
  • 2 chilli (finely chopped)
  • 2 clove garlic (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp coriander (chopped)
  • ½ onion (finely chopped)
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp cumin powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (crushed)
  • 2 to mato (chopped)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp coriander (chopped)

Steps

  1. firstly, grate 200 grams paneer and keep aside.
  2. in a large pan heat 2 tsp oil, 2 chilli, 2 clove garlic, and 2 tbsp coriander.
  3. add ½ onion and saute until the onions shrink slightly.
  4. keeping the flame on low add ¼ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp coriander powder, ½ tsp cumin powder, ½ tsp garam masala, and 1 tsp kasuri methi. saute until the spices turn aromatic.
  5. now add 2 tomato and saute well.
  6. mash smooth making sure everything is well combined.
  7. further add grated paneer, ½ tsp salt, and mix well.
  8. add 1 cup water and mix well adjusting consistency as required.
  9. now add 1 tbsp butter, 2 tbsp coriander, and simmer for 2 minutes.
  10. mix well making sure everything is well combined.
  11. finally, enjoy paneer ghotala with buttery pav.
